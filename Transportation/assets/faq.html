FAQ

What is "Transportation app" about?

Transportation is a travel guide app that allows you to quickly search for transport options based on your desired route, your price range and departure date.

How to use this app?

You must select your route, departure date and price range. You can also include your through points in your route. Then you can search for buses,micros,planes or all vehicles that travels through that route meeting all criterias.

What are the key features?

-View transit schedules and route itineraries (even without an internet connection!)
- Star your favorite routes for instant access to upcoming departures.
-Know at glance about different vehicles travelling in same route satisfying our criterias.
- Narrow your search by sorting list by time and price.

What are the transportation types

-Bus
-Micro
-Plane
